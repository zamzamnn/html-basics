LIFE AFTER HIGH SCHOOL

Life after high school marks a significant transition, filled with new opportunities and challenges. Whether you choose to pursue higher education, vocational training, or enter the workforce, this period involves making important decisions about your future. If you opt for college or university, prepare for a more independent lifestyle, where time management and self-discipline are crucial.
